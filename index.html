We strive to see a future free of environmental problems

Our Goals

Donations

We want to put an end to the behaviour that damages our environment, and to fix the consequences of these behaviours by gathering donations to charities that are actively providing to this cause, and by gathering volunteers to fix certain issues through groupwork.

Community

We endeavour to create a community of individuals who have sufficient knowledge and information about the negative impact on the environment to feel responsible and be aware of how their behaviours impact the environment as a whole and how they could help it.

Marine Life

The main issues our team addresses are climate change and marine pollution, marine pollution has become one of the biggest threats that face our earth, this pollution causes harm to the environment, to the survival of all species, and to worldwide economic systems.

A better world

Many living organisms are affected by marine pollution and thus directly affecting the food chain, resulting in major food insecurity in certain regions around the world, poorer people will be the hardest-hit by this impact, we want to prevent this from happening.

Coastal Areas

Coastal regions worldwide face numerous environmental threats from soil erosion, overexploitation, industrial wastes and more, 15% of the world's plastic waste end up on beaches. Marine pollution can endanger coastal communities' livelihoods and associated industries, pose a risk to human health via polluted seafood, and impact marine ecosystems.

Marine Life

Marine life is massively affected by pollution and plastic waste. When examined, All baby sea turtles, 59% of whales are affected by plastic pollution, Plastic is found in third of the fish captured for human consumption, while more than two-thirds of the world's fish stocks suffer from plastic ingestion. Over a million seabirds and 100,000 marine animals die annually due to plastic marine pollution

Plastic Waste

The world produces 381 million tonnes of plastic a year. 79% of plastic waste are either dumped into the ocean or landfills, with a total of 8 millon tonnes of plastic dumped into the ocean every year. Only 269,000 tonnes of plastic float on the surface while 165 million tonnes circulates deep in earth's marine system
